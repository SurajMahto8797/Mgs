ABOUT US

MGS Electronix is an Electronics services based company incorporated in 2018 as LED lighting solutions provider Headquartered in Jamshedpur, Jharkhand . Started with LED lighting solutions now we have entered in the field of IoT. Having built an exceptionally talented team of hardware and software engineers, MGS now has a suite of technology solutions in the fields of Analog, Digital and Embedded Systems for IoT applications. We always look forward to work on the challenging projects.
MGS is not just a name; it’s a Faith which we gain from our customers by delivering best Services . So, our slogan is Your Faith, Our Service .

Our vision:- To transform the spaces around our consumers by providing best designed LED lights. We always try to remove the darkness from our consumer’s house and transform into heaven by spreading lights.

Our mission:- To become a family member of every house on the planet by enlighten the house with our LEDs and designed solutions.

Core values:- Our core values are Integrity, Excellence,Team work, Empowerment, Friendliness and Sharing & Caring.

Our promises :-We believe in adding value to the world through our efficient designed solutions, making the planet healthier and more sustainable that contribute to social as well as ecological well-being.
